How to Use the Shift Report

  1. Upload Line Transactions: Select your "Line Transaction" CSV file. This contains all itemized sales, discounts, and voids.
  2. Upload Payments: Select your "Payments" CSV file.
  3. Filter by Staff: Once both files are uploaded, use the Staff dropdown to isolate a specific person's performance.
  4. Analyze Data:
    • Sales Table: View "Own Sales" vs "Made by Others". Numerical cells have a solid background for better readability.
    • Payments Table: See exact payments collected by that staff member, filtered to match the accounts from the sales data.
  5. Calculation Logic: The top green total calculates your net performance: Final Price + Voids - Comps - Discounts.
